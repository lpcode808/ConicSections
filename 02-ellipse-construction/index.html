Teaching Notes

Core Idea

An ellipse is the set of all points where d(P,F1)+d(P,F2) is constant.

Try This

Drag one focus closer to center and predict how the ellipse width and height change.

Teacher Prompt

Ask students to locate where the values of a and b are visible in the shape.

Vocabulary

  • Major axis: longest diameter.
  • Minor axis: shortest diameter.
  • Foci: two defining points of ellipse.
